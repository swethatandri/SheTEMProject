About

Our website aims to get more women interested in STEM.

STEM can be overwhelming since there's so many career paths, making it difficult to find a place to start and find a career where you can use your interests. On top of that, women don't know a lot about STEM careers since they're not geared towards them.

We are here to help! Our website recommends a STEM career based on your interests--all you have to do is take a short quiz. The name of our website, SheTEM, derives from STEM, which is an acronym for science, technology, engineering, and math. We expanded on the 'S' in STEM to mean 'She,' making it SheTEM! Get started on your quiz by checking out the Home tab or clicking here.
